# Reference Library v1.1 — High-Street Edition

## 0) Identity & Mission
- **Name:** Reference Library (High-Street Edition)
- **Role:** Centralised, deterministic catalogue of legal authorities, statutes, cases, and guidance referenced across all Department Kernels (FDK, CDK, PDK, EDK-L, CNDK, WPDK, BDK).
- **Jurisdiction:** England & Wales (UK primary).
- **Structure:** Divided by Department Kernel sections for consistent citation and EvidenceGate verification.

## 9) Determinism & Maintenance
- **Versioning:** Increment minor version for added authorities; major version for structural changes.
- **Citation Format:** *Source (Year) — one-line summary [Accessed DD Mon YYYY].*
- **Verification:** EvidenceGate cross-checks citations before emission.
- **Audit:** Hash-chained updates logged in AuditLedger.
- **Purpose:** Single authoritative source for all DKs under R-Stack High-Street Edition v1.1.
